Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to create a fun way to learn church history through the jeoparady format

Audience

Seminary/Institute students and teachers looking for a fun way to learn church history

Dynamic elements

The dynamic elements will be clicking on the panel to expand the jeoparady question, adding and subtracting points based on the user response, and adding a timer to encourage quick reponses and subtracting points if the player does not get the answer right. There will also be a daily double where the player can bet any number of points and they'll win or lose that amount of points based on their answer
